Who we are?

Highway Constructions, was established during the year 1980-81, to undertake construction projects in the field of Roads & Bridges. The firm is registered with Pakistan Engineering Council in Category C-A (No Limit) having license No.0203. The firm is owned and managed by eminent professionals having vast experience in planning and executing civil engi-neering works in the field of Highway. Sewerage and Bridges, thus contribut-ing best services towards Construction / Improvement of Communications network in the Province of Punjab.
